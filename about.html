About Us

Our Mission

At Health & Wellness, we want to make it easier for people to live healthier lives. We know it can be overwhelming knowing where to start, so we try to keep things simple and practical.

We share information on fitness, nutrition, and mindfulness that is easy to understand and actually useful in day to day life.

Our Core Values

  • Honesty — We only share health information that is backed up by research.
  • Inclusivity — Our resources are for everyone, no matter your age or fitness level.
  • Balance — We believe in looking after both your body and your mind equally.

Meet the Team

Our team includes nutritionists, fitness coaches and health professionals who help create the content on this site.

Did You Know?

According to the World Health Organization, adults should aim for at least 150–300 minutes of moderate-intensity aerobic activity per week for significant health benefits.

Our Story

Health & Wellness started in 2020 as a small blog and has grown a lot since then. What began as a way to share simple health tips has turned into a full website covering fitness, nutrition and wellbeing.

We are proud to support our community on their wellness journeys every day. Get in touch if you’d like to work with us or have any questions.
