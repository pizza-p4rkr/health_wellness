Wellness Tips

  • Eat a rainbow — different coloured vegitables provide different nutrients.
  • Aim for 8 cups (2 litres) of water per day.
  • Rest days are just as important as workout days.
  • Protein helps repair muscle — aim for 1.2–2g per kg of body weight.
  • Sleep 7–9 hours per night for optimal recovery.

Understanding Macronutrients

Protein builds and repairs muscle and supports immune function. Found in meat, fish, eggs, legumes, and dairy.

Carbohydrates are your body’s primary energy source. Choose complex carbs like oats, brown rice, and sweet potato.

Fats support brain health and hormone production. Choose healthy fats from avocado, nuts, and olive oil.

Hydration Guide

Staying hydrated improves energy, concentration, and physical performance. Increase intake on hot days and during exercise.
